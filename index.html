Professionalism Policy

Rule 1

Students will be productive and work oriented.

Rule 2

Students will take care of their equipment and will not abuse technology.

Rule 3

Students will be respectful to each other and web design clients.

Rule 4

Students will not have food or drink in the classroom.

Rule 5

Students will maintain a clean workspace and keep cords neat.

Rule 6

Students will bring their laptop everyday to class.

Rule 7

Students will turn their projects in on time.

Rule 8

Students will not distract each other.

Rule 9

Students will be on time to class and maintain good attendance to school.

Rule 10

Students will be responsible for work missed on days they are absent.
